About me!

I am an embedded system software architect and lead with more than 12 years of experience in both software and hardware for ARM and DSP multi-core microprocessor based mobile/consumer handheld embedded platforms and commercial phones available today in market . At my work, I have gained hands-on expertise at board bring up and porting of boot loaders, kernel, HLOS(Android and ChromeOS) and toolchains. I have extensive experience on full product development where I have worked on device driver, HAL, system frameworks, firmware development, validation and Integration on Linux(android, chromium OS), windows CE and windows mobile (5.0, 6.1,6.5 and WP7.0) and RTOS.


I have strong expertise in battery energy and life prediction for mobile devices, mobile device power modeling, application profiling and energy bug detection, power management implementation and optimization for mobile hand-held devices. I also have hands on experience on debugging and bug fix of display, framebuffer, graphics, TVOUT driver on Android, multimedia driver(Video-H.264) and test component development and validation on WinCE, speech codecs optimization on DSPs, verified boot (boot loader and kernel security) features using SHA and RSA algorithms key signing and android SElinux.


I have developed, managed and lead teams and worked on a large complex project with a team size of more than 100 engineers with excellent working experience in agile and lean scrum methodologies where I have gained expertise in the areas of project proposals, proof of concepts and demos, effort and cost management, resource management, client, vendor and cross team management and technical documentations(specifications, design, test and release). I also have experience in product release management and DevOps where I have worked on setting up the GIT server, Gitolite, and Gerrit as early project setups and Continuous Integrations(CI) flows using a Jankins, electric commander, git and perforce.


Recently, due to personal Interest of development in web technologies and embedded android development, I have also worked and gained experience in bootstrap, HTML5, CSS, javascript, XML, JSON, RESTful api, apache, python, perl and shell scripting and android app development and contributed to open source community based on Odroid development hardware.
