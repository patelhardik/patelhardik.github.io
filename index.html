About me!

I am an embedded system software architect and lead with more than 12 years of experience in both software and hardware for ARM and DSP multi-core microprocessor based mobile/consumer handheld embedded platforms and many commercial phones available today in market. My area of expertise are board bring up and porting, development, integration, bugfix and validation of bootloader, kernel, HLOS(Android, ChroiumOS, WinCE) and RTOS.

I have extensive experience on full product development where I have developed, managed and lead teams and worked on a large complex project with a team size of more than 100 engineers with excellent working experience in agile and lean scrum methodologies. I have also gained expertise in the areas of project proposals, proof of concepts and demos, early project setups, resources, effort and cost management, client/vendor and cross team management, technical documentations(specifications, design, test and release), product release managment and Continuous Integrations(CI) flows.
